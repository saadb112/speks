WHAT IS CUSTOM PATCH?

Custom patches or badges are created using fabric backing and thread to form an embroidered design. This traditional art form was originally done by hand, but now it can be machine-stitched or embroidered with the help of computers. Embroidered patches are versatile and can be used for a variety of purposes, including branding, identification, and decoration.
